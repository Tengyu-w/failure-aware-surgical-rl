## Evidence Folders

- `tables/`: CSV summaries and scored results.
- `figures/`: plots for recovery, routing, embeddings, and proxy experiments.
- `media/`: SurRoL rendered rollout GIF/MP4/PNG evidence.
- `archive/legacy_round_reports/`: preserved intermediate round reports.
- `archive/supporting_materials/`: earlier pitches, stage notes, and supporting
  datasets that are not part of the first reading path.

The archive is intentionally preserved for detail and provenance, but the main
research story is the ordered sequence in [docs/research_sequence.md](../docs/research_sequence.md).
